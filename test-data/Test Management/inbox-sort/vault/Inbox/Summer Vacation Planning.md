# Summer Vacation Planning 2026

## Constraints

- Two weeks in July or August (school holidays)
- Budget: max 3,500 EUR for family of four
- Kids (8 and 11) want beach AND mountains (compromise needed)
- No more than 6 hours driving from home

## Options

### Option A: Lake Garda, Italy

- Beach + mountains in one place
- ~5 hours drive
- Apartment rental: ~1,200 EUR for 2 weeks
- Lots of kid-friendly activities (Gardaland, windsurfing courses)
- Downside: crowded in August

### Option B: Croatian Coast (Istria)

- Beautiful beaches, less crowded than Italy
- ~7 hours drive (slightly over limit)
- Cheaper: apartment ~900 EUR for 2 weeks
- Less mountain hiking nearby
- Kids loved it last time (2024)

### Option C: Austrian Alps + Lake Constance

- Mountains first week, lake second week
- ~3 hours drive
- More expensive: two rentals at ~800 EUR each
- Great hiking but water might be cold for kids

## Decision Needed By

April 15 — popular apartments book out fast in summer.
