# Morning Routine Overhaul

Current routine is not working. Hitting snooze until 7:30, skipping breakfast, arriving at the desk groggy. Time to redesign.

## New Target Schedule

- 6:00 — Wake up, no phone for first 30 minutes
- 6:15 — Stretch or yoga (10 minutes, follow YouTube routine)
- 6:30 — Cold shower (start warm, end cold — 30 seconds minimum)
- 6:45 — Breakfast: oatmeal with banana and walnuts, black coffee
- 7:15 — Journal: three things I am grateful for, one intention for the day
- 7:30 — Walk to the office or start commute

## Rules

- No screens before 7:00 (alarm clock, not phone)
- Prepare oatmeal ingredients the night before
- Lay out workout clothes before bed
- Track consistency in habit tracker app (streaks matter)

## Why This Matters

Sleep research says the first 90 minutes after waking set the tone for the day. Cortisol peaks naturally at sunrise — riding that wave instead of fighting it with caffeine should improve focus and mood.

## Review Date

Check in after 2 weeks. If compliance is below 70%, simplify the routine instead of abandoning it.
