# Home Office Desk Setup Research

Current setup is causing neck pain after long sessions. Time to upgrade.

## Standing Desk Options

| # | Model | Price | Height Range | Notes |
|---|-------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | IKEA Bekant | 499 EUR | 65-125 cm | Basic, reliable, 10-year warranty |
| 2 | Flexispot E7 | 479 EUR | 58-123 cm | Better motor, memory presets |
| 3 | Autonomous SmartDesk | 399 EUR | 73-124 cm | Budget option, slower motor |

Leaning toward Flexispot E7 — the memory presets are worth it for switching between sitting and standing throughout the day.

## Monitor

Currently using laptop screen only. Need an external monitor.

- 27-inch, 4K, USB-C with power delivery (charges laptop through one cable)
- Dell U2723QE: ~520 EUR, great reviews for text clarity
- LG 27UP850: ~430 EUR, slightly worse color accuracy but cheaper

## Chair

- Herman Miller Aeron (used): ~800 EUR — the gold standard
- Autonomous ErgoChair: ~350 EUR — decent budget option
- Keep current chair and add a lumbar support cushion: ~40 EUR

## Total Budget

Aiming for under 1,500 EUR. Desk + monitor + chair cushion for now. Real ergonomic chair later when budget allows.
